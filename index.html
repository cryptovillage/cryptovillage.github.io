DEF CON Crypto & Privacy Village

At the DEF CON Crypto & Privacy village you can learn how to secure your own systems, while also picking up some tips and tricks on how to break classical and modern encryption. Workshops will be available on everything from GPG to FOIA, code sprints on security tools, crypto-related games, and talks on privacy from EFF staffers and more.
